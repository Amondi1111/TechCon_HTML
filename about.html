History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a small gathering of local tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has evolved into a global event, attracting thousands of developers, entrepreneurs, and innovators each year.

OMission

At TechCon, our mission is to drive innovation, foster collaboration, and empower the next generation of technology leaders. We believe in creating inclusive spaces where ideas can flourish and groundbreaking solutions emerge.
